Chilli (con carne)

A delicious and healthy bowl perfect for large families or meal-prep. It's packed with (hidden) vegtables, high in nutrients and protien- get creative and add your own choice of toppings!"

Ingredients

  • 200g ground beef (or any other alternative)
  • 2 x Pepper/Capsicum
  • 1 x Tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 1 x Large onion
  • 1 x Courgette
  • 1 x Aubergine
  • 1 x Tinned sweetcorn
  • 1 x Packet of chilli seasoning
  • 3 or 4 sections of garlic
  • 1 x packet of tortilla wrap OR serve with rice

Toppings (optional but recommended to add a few of these for optimal taste)

  • Grated cheese of choice
  • Ripe avocado
  • Corriander
  • Sour Cream
  • Gherkins
  • Nachos
  • Jalapenos/Chillies
  • 1 x Lemon/lime
  • Chorizo

Preparation

  1. Chop all vegetables into small cubes- this makes it easier to break down
  2. Heat pan with oil and fry onions until slighly brown. Immediately add the ground beef (or alternative) and cook for a few minutes, stirring everything once ina while to make sure the protein is crumbly.
  3. Once the protein looks cooked, slowly fold in the peppers, the courgette and aubergine. Leave for two minutes and stir - repeat 3 times.
  4. Now you can add your tinned items, the beans, the sweecorn and the tinned tomatoes. Also add the seasoning, salt and garlic. Stir well.
  5. Now the bulk is done, just let it simmer on low heat for 20/30 mins (your choice) with a lid on. Remember to check every 5/6 mins and also give it a good stir.
  6. After the 20/30 min, taste and add more salt and some black pepper to your taste.

Serving: as a burrito

  1. Get a tortilla wrap and place chilli you just made in the middle. leave the bottom 1/5th empty as you may want to fold it up to prevent leaking at the bottom.
  2. Layer in your toppings, like sliced avocade, gherkins, lettuce, cheese. Maybe you want to add some sour cream, and crumble on some nachos for a crunchy bite? It's up to you
  3. Fold in the bottom empty section towards the middle of the wrap, then fold in the sides so it looks like a wrap and ENJOY!.

Serving- with rice

  1. Add some rice to bowl and top with chilli
  2. Same as above, add your toppings. I recommed, avocado, gherkins, cheese, lettuce and some pan-fried chorizo
